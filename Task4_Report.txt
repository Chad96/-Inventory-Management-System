User Guide
Setup

Open index.html in a modern web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).
Ensure an internet connection for loading Tailwind CSS.
No additional setup is required as the prototype uses in-memory data.

Usage

Add a Product:
In the "Manage Products" section, enter the product name, description, price, and stock threshold.
Click "Add Product" to save the product.


Update Stock:
In the "Manage Stock" section, select a product from the dropdown.
Enter the new stock quantity and click "Update Stock".


Generate Sales Report:
In the "Sales Report" section, click "Generate Report" to view sales from the last 30 days.


View Notifications:
Check the "Notifications" section for low stock alerts, updated automatically when stock changes.



Notes

The prototype uses in-memory data, so refreshing the page resets all data.
Ensure valid numerical inputs for price, threshold, and quantity to avoid errors.
